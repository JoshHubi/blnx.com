Broad digital media technology. Across four screens.

blinkx is a broad digital media technology, distribution and monetization platform that connects consumers, advertisers and content across four screens. At its foundation Blinkx is powered by CORE, the world’s most advanced video engine, conceived at Cambridge University, enhanced by $150M in R&D over 15 years, and protected by 111 patents.
